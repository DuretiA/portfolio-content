Welcome to my travel diary!


On this website I'll be posting about my recent out of country travels and documnting the trip details. I'll be sharing reccomendations as well as things I learned on the trip.
As an avid traveller it's nice I heavily research places I want to visit before I go and I want to start storing all that information I learn before/after the trip somewhere
where others could benefit from them.

we were able to find cheap flights. This was my first trip out the country since the pandemic. Right before the pandemic I was on a Euro/North african-tour that was partially
solo. I was able to visit (Morocco, Spain, Portugal, France and Italy). A few months before my euro tour I was also able to study abroad. During my study abroad in Indonesia,
I was able to visit, Thailand and Malaysia. I try to make the most out of a trip by visiting neighboring countries if possible.
